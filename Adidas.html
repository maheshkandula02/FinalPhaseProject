-Adidas famous for

Adidas AG (German: [ˈʔadiˌdas]; stylized as ɑdidɑs since 1949) is a German multinational corporation,
founded and headquartered in Herzogenaurach, Germany, that designs and manufactures shoes,
clothing and accessories. It is the largest sportswear manufacturer in Europe, and the
second largest in the world, after Nike.

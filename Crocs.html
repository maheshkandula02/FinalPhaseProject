CROCS

Crocs plan to continue to operate leases abut their retail stores. They want to reduce the cost so they also continue to assess the possibilities of cost reduction.
Their assessment of possibilities of cost reduction will involve an evaluation of contracts for sponsorships,
operating lease contracts and other contracts that require future minimum payments resulting in fixed operating costs.
Any changes to these contracts may require stop the supply of fees or other charges in advance that could cost large numbers of money.

Spykar

Spykar Lifestyles will foray into footwear in 2017 in a bid to strengthen its portfolio comprising men’s and women’s clothing and accessories, COO Sanjay Vakharia said.
"We will launch casual footwear under our parent brand to complete the wardrobe of the Indian consumer,” Vakharia said. Spykar offers garment and accessories to young adults.
Spykar got acquired by Bagri Group in 2014. Earlier this year, the company announced its decision to move away from being a just a menswear brand
and launched womenswear in August. The company manufactures 1.3 million units of denims per year and is growing 30% y-o-y, he said.
The company has not yet divulged the investments on the proposed footwear project. “We would outsource manufacturing and keep the price points between Rs 600 and Rs 2500,” he added.
